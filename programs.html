Affordable Dental Care Program

Ensuring everyone has access to quality dental care.

Who Can Apply?

Our program is designed to assist uninsured or underinsured residents of Northumberland, Union, and Snyder Counties who meet specific income guidelines.

We believe that financial hardship should not prevent you from maintaining a healthy smile.

Benefits of the Program

  • ✓ Reduced fees on preventive care (cleanings, exams)
  • ✓ Discounted restorative treatments (fillings, extractions)
  • ✓ Priority booking for emergency dental care

Application Steps

  1. Download the application form using the link below.
  2. Complete the form with accurate household income details.
  3. Attach required proof of income (recent tax return or pay stubs).
  4. Submit the packet at our clinic or via mail to our address.
