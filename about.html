About Us

Dedicated to serving Northumberland, Union and Snyder County residents.

Our Mission

Susquehanna River Valley Dental Health Clinic is a community dental clinic with a mission to provide patient-centered, affordable dentistry to families and individuals in our area.

We understand that access to quality dental care is essential for overall health, which is why we break down barriers to treatment and focus on comprehensive, compassionate care.
